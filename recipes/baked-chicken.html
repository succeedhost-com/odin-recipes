Baked Chicken Recipe

Juicy Oven-Baked Chicken Breast — Simple, Flavorful, and Tender | By: your's truly :)

This oven-baked chicken breast recipe is an easy, healthy, and foolproof way to make juicy, flavorful chicken in under 40 minutes. Lightly seasoned with herbs and spices, this dish pairs beautifully with roasted veggies, rice, or a fresh salad. Great for meal prep or a simple weeknight dinner!


 

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon light brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning (or mix of oregano & basil)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. If needed, gently pound the chicken breasts to even thickness for consistent cooking.
  3. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels.
  4. Drizzle olive oil over both sides of the chicken and rub to coat evenly.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  6. Rub the seasoning mix evenly on both sides of the chicken breasts.
  7. Place the chicken in a baking dish or on a baking sheet in a single layer.
  8. Bake for 18–25 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  9. Optional: Broil for 2–3 minutes at the end for a golden, caramelized top (watch carefully!).
  10. Remove from the oven, tent loosely with foil, and let rest for 5–10 minutes before slicing or serving.
